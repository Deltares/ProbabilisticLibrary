How to Contribute
-----------------

Sharing your work helps grow the community and improves visibility of the
Probabilistic Suite across the water sector and beyond. A contribution can be:

- A Jupyter notebook with a self-contained example
- A short write-up with code snippets describing how you applied the library
- A link to a published paper or report that used the Probabilistic Suite

To submit, open a discussion on
`GitHub <https://github.com/Deltares/ProbabilisticLibrary/discussions>`_
with the tag **[showcase]**, or email
`d-prob-community@deltares.nl <mailto:d-prob-community@deltares.nl>`_.
We will review and add accepted contributions to this page.
